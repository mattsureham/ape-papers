# Internal Review: apep_0642 v3

## Reviewer 2 (Harsh)
The reparameterized τ=-0.072 (p≈0) is robust and survives stacking (-0.067, p≈0). This is a genuine finding. However: balance test still fails (F=26.4), individual air releases are flat, and the mechanism interaction is null (p=0.44). The "composition illusion" framing is creative but the paper must not overclaim that inspections cause this — the composition shift may reflect mean reversion, correlated enforcement, or selection.

## Editor (Constructive)
The V3 is a substantial improvement over V2. The reparameterization makes the estimand clean, the stacked design addresses TWFE concerns, and the 17-year panel restores power. The composition-bias framing is novel and defensible. The water-decline finding (-0.029, p=0.006) with CWA controls is a genuine methodological contribution.
