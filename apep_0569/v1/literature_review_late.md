## Contribution Gap

This paper fills a specific gap: while the pass-through literature documents heterogeneity across firms (Amiti et al. 2014) and invoice currencies (Gopinath et al. 2010), no paper has systematically documented pass-through heterogeneity along the value chain within a single country episode using product-level data. The Egypt setting provides a uniquely clean shock for this purpose.

## Overlap Risk: LOW

No published paper studies Egypt's 2016 devaluation with HS6-level Comtrade data classified by BEC categories. The closest related work is on Turkey's 2018 currency crisis, but that literature focuses on firm-level responses rather than product-level value chain heterogeneity.
