## Novelty Assessment

**Does any published paper since the early lit review replicate our exact design?**

No. No published paper applies the examiner-IV design to Y02 green/clean energy patents using application-level data (grants + abandonments). The closest work remains Sampat & Williams (2019) on gene patents and Galasso & Schankerman (2015) on patent invalidation.

**Has our contribution claim shifted?**

Yes — appropriately. The contribution is now framed as:
1. **Methodological:** Demonstrating that PatEx application-level data enables proper first-stage estimation in examiner IV designs (impossible with grants-only data)
2. **Descriptive:** Mixed downstream evidence — significant at subclass-year collapse, null at AU-year collapse — honestly presented as inconclusive

This is a more modest but more honest contribution than v1's "blocking effect" claim.
