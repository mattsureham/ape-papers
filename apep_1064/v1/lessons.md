## Discovery
- **Idea selected:** idea_1836 — Brazil's Pix instant payment system and microenterprise formalization
- **Data source:** IBGE SIDRA (CEMPRE, Census 2010, population estimates) — all API-accessible; BCB Pix municipal data was NOT available despite manifest claim
- **Key risk:** Urbanization as treatment intensity proxy is correlated with many development dimensions beyond digital readiness

## Execution
- **What worked:** Extended panel (2015-2021) resolved the pre-trend concern — placebo timing test went from significant (p=0.006 with 4 years) to null (p=0.27 with 7 years). State × year FE is the strongest specification.
- **What didn't:** The original idea called for municipal-level Pix transaction data and monthly MEI registrations from Mapa de Empresas — neither was accessible via public API. ANATEL mobile density data was also inaccessible. Had to pivot to Census urbanization as the instrument and annual CEMPRE data as the outcome.
- **Review feedback adopted:** Softened causal claims about Pix mechanism; expanded event study to show all 6 pre-treatment years; acknowledged that urbanization captures multiple development dimensions.
