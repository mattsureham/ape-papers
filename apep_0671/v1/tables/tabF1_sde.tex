{\footnotesize \emph{Notes:} This table reports standardized effect sizes (SDE) to facilitate cross-study comparison of treatment effect magnitudes. For continuous treatments, SDE $= \hat{\beta} \times \text{SD}(X) / \text{SD}(Y)$, which gives the effect of a one-standard-deviation increase in county-level restricted-origin foreign-born share, measured in standard deviations of the outcome.
