## Contribution Language

1. First application of heterogeneity-robust staggered DiD to ERPO–suicide question with longest available panel (1999–2024)
2. Demonstrates TWFE produces qualitatively misleading estimate (sign flip)
3. Directly tests means substitution by decomposing suicide into firearm/non-firearm components
