# Research Ideas

## Idea 1: Perplexity in Congress — Habermas Meets Shannon (SELECTED)

Train a GPT language model from scratch on 30 years of U.S. Congressional floor debate and use perplexity as a measurement instrument for deliberation quality. Decompose into conditional perplexity, marginal perplexity, and the Deliberation Index.

**Feasibility:** High — public data (GovInfo, HuggingFace), open-source framework (nanochat), runs on consumer hardware.
**Novelty:** High — first autoregressive LM trained from scratch on legislative text for measurement.
**Identification:** Institutional design (House vs Senate rules) provides exogenous variation.

See: `projects/congressional_lm/PAPER_IDEA.md`
