- **Why this is not duplicative:** No NBER papers study the 2014 QPV/ZUS zone redesignation in France. While place-based policies are well-studied (Busso et al. 2013, Kline & Moretti 2014 on US enterprise zones), the *removal* of place-based designation is almost never studied.
