\textit{Notes:} Two-way fixed effects estimates of the effect of losing ZRR designation on FN/RN first-round presidential vote share.
Treatment group: communes that lost ZRR status under the 2015 legislative reform (identified from the 2014 vs.\ 2018 official classification lists; administrative reclassification effective 2017--2018). Control group: communes that retained ZRR status throughout.
Post $= \mathbb{1}[\text{year} = 2022]$. Although the legislative reform was enacted in 2015 and administrative reclassification occurred in 2017--2018, transition provisions extended ZRR benefits through 2020; the 2022 election is the first where the full economic effects would have been felt. Column (1) is the baseline specification.
