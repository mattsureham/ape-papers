## Assessment:
The paper's literature coverage is strong for the energy efficiency and housing literature. The main gap identified by reviewers is the modern DiD/event-study methodology literature (Roth 2022, FHS 2019) and RDD-under-manipulation literature (Gerard et al. 2020). These are methodological rather than substantive gaps and would strengthen the paper but are not fatal omissions given the paper's honest treatment of its limitations.
