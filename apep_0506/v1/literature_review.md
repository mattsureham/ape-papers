- **Why this is not duplicative:** The RDD literature on Indian elections has studied criminality (Prakash et al. 2019 JDE), education (Jain et al. 2023 JCE), and party alignment (Asher & Novosad 2017 AEJ:Applied), but NEVER wealth as the main treatment variable.
