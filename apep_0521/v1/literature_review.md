## Selection Decision (Required)

- **Selected idea title:** When the Checkpoint Vanishes: Constitutional Carry Laws, Gun Violence, and Police Safety
- **Why this is not duplicative:** The NBER literature on gun laws focuses on shall-issue (RTC) laws (Donohue, Aneja, Weber 2019, NBER WP 30190). Constitutional carry — removing the permit requirement entirely — is a distinct deregulation margin that has NOT been studied with modern staggered DiD methods across 22+ states. The police safety (LEOKA) angle has no precedent in the economics literature.
- **Specific contribution delta (1-2 sentences):** We provide the first comprehensive causal estimate of constitutional carry (as distinct from shall-issue) on firearm mortality, violent crime, and law enforcement officer safety using Callaway-Sant'Anna estimators across 22+ treated states from 2000-2023. The multi-margin design (civilian deaths by intent + police deaths + crime + gun demand) with built-in non-firearm placebos and welfare quantification represents a fundamentally different scope from the single-margin RTC literature.
