## Reviewer 1 (GPT-5.2): MAJOR REVISION

**1. Panel A/B joint identification**: We agree that Panel B should not be presented as a clean mechanism validation of Panel A's effect. **5. Welfare sensitivity**: We now report a range using both TWFE ($3.9B) and SA ($1.1B) point estimates.

**6. Mechanism language**: Softened throughout from "confirms" to "consistent with."
