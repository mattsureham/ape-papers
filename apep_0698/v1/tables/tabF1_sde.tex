\textit{Notes:} Standardized effect sizes (SDE $= \hat{\beta}/\text{SD}(Y)$) for cross-study comparison. SD($X$) marked ``---'' for binary treatments. SD($Y$) is unconditional (Table~\ref{tab:summary}).
\textbf{Country:} United States.
\textbf{Research question:} Does PPP Second Draw eligibility at the 25\% revenue decline threshold preserve nonprofit employment?
\textbf{Policy mechanism:} PPP Second Draw (Jan 2021) provided forgivable loans to organizations with 25\% quarterly revenue decline, capped at 2.5$\times$ monthly payroll.
\textbf{Outcome:} Log W-3 employee count (Form 990); growth $= (\text{Emp}_{21} - \text{Emp}_{19})/\text{Emp}_{19}$.
\textbf{Treatment:} Binary: crossing 25\% annual decline threshold (RDD) or receiving Second Draw (OLS).
\textbf{Data:} IRS SOI 990 (2018--2023) linked to SBA PPP FOIA via BMF; 158,232 orgs.
\textbf{Method:} Local linear RDD, CCT bandwidth (rows 1--3); OLS, state + size FE (row 4).
\textbf{Sample:} 990 filers 2019--2020, positive employment, $\geq$\$10K revenue.
Thresholds: large neg ($<-0.15$), mod neg ($-0.15$ to $-0.05$), small neg ($-0.05$ to $-0.005$), null ($\pm 0.005$), small pos ($0.005$ to $0.05$), mod pos ($0.05$ to $0.15$), large pos ($>0.15$). Classification by magnitude, not significance.
