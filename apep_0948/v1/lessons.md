## Discovery
- **Idea selected:** idea_1808 — Triplicate IV for opioid supply → Medicaid MAT demand. QJE-published instrument gives instant credibility.
- **Data source:** ARCOS (Azure, 178M transactions) + T-MSIS (local, 227M claims). Both worked perfectly.
- **Key risk:** Only 7 triplicate states for cross-sectional IV. Precision concern proved justified.

## Execution
- **What worked:** Oxycodone-specific supply (not total pills) is critical — F went from 0.64 to 15.3. The drug-level decomposition is the key methodological insight.
- **What didn't:** State-level cross-section is too imprecise for this instrument. County-level panel is the clear V2 path.
- **Review feedback adopted:** Strengthened limitations discussion, fixed abstract data count, clarified F-statistic interpretation, added exclusion restriction caveats about intermediate policies.
- **Placebo success:** Non-opioid SUD coefficient = 0.009 (p=0.99) — strongest evidence for the exclusion restriction.
