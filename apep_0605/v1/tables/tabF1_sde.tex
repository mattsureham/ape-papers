{\footnotesize \emph{Notes:} This table reports standardized effect sizes (SDE) to facilitate cross-study comparison. SDE $= \hat{\beta} / \text{SD}(Y)$ for binary treatment. SD($Y$) is the unconditional standard deviation of the log outcome.

\textbf{Research question:} Effect of shale play exposure (hydraulic fracturing boom) on county-level employment and earnings.
\textbf{Treatment:} Binary indicator for county overlying a major US shale play.
\textbf{Data:} Census QWI (LEHD), 2001--2023, county-year panel.
\textbf{Method:} Staggered DiD with Callaway--Sant'Anna (2021) estimator, state-clustered standard errors.
\textbf{Sample:} 3,140 counties, 71,408 county-years.

Classification thresholds: large negative ($< -0.15$), moderate negative ($-0.15$ to $-0.05$), small negative ($-0.05$ to $-0.005$), null ($-0.005$ to $0.005$), small positive ($0.005$ to $0.05$), moderate positive ($0.05$ to $0.15$), large positive ($> 0.15$).
