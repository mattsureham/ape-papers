# Research Ideas

## Idea 1: Tightening the Gate: How the EU Mortgage Credit Directive Reshaped Housing Finance Across Europe
**Policy:** EU Mortgage Credit Directive (2014/17/EU), adopted February 2014, transposition deadline 21 March 2016. Key provisions: mandatory creditworthiness assessment before granting mortgage credit (not relying predominantly on property value), European Standardised Information Sheet (ESIS) disclosure, conduct standards for mortgage intermediaries, restrictions on tying practices. Massively staggered transposition: Estonia transposed March 2015, Spain not until June 2019. Only 8 of 28 member states transposed on time. Commission launched infringement proceedings against late transposers.
**Outcome:** (1) Mortgage lending rates — ECB MIR dataset, monthly annualized agreed rates on new housing loans, 21 reporting countries. (2) Mortgage lending volumes — ECB MIR, monthly new business volumes for housing loans. (3) Housing price index — Eurostat prc_hpi_q, quarterly (2015=100), 27 EU member states, 2005-2025. Built-in placebo: consumer credit rates (not covered by MCD).
**Identification:** Staggered DiD using Callaway & Sant'Anna (2021). Treatment: binary indicator for full MCD transposition notification from CELLAR SPARQL (375 NIMs across 28 countries). 24 countries with transposition dates spanning March 2015 to March 2019 — 4 full years of staggered variation. 11 years (44 quarters) of pre-treatment data for parallel trends testing. Robustness: Bacon decomposition, Sun-Abraham estimator, event-study plots, leave-one-out, randomization inference.
**Why it's novel:** Zero published papers use the MCD's staggered transposition as a causal identification strategy. The existing MCD literature is purely legal/descriptive. The Commission's 2021 evaluation describes transposition but conducts no econometric analysis. This is the first causal evaluation of the EU's flagship consumer mortgage regulation.
**Feasibility check:** Variation confirmed (24 countries, March 2015 to March 2019 from CELLAR SPARQL). Data confirmed (ECB MIR 2,592 country-month obs; Eurostat HPI 1,992 country-quarter obs; no API keys needed). Novelty confirmed (no causal papers). Pre-COVID timing (treatment window 2015-2019) avoids the COVID confounder that plagues post-2020 EU directives.
