## Contribution Positioning
The paper clearly identifies its contribution: "first causal estimates of PDMP mandates on higher education outcomes." This is verified — no prior study examines this specific policy-outcome combination. The closest work (Zuo 2022) is correlational and examines opioid exposure broadly, not PDMP mandates specifically. The contribution paragraph in the introduction successfully differentiates from three literatures: (1) PDMP evaluation, (2) opioid crisis consequences, and (3) determinants of college completion.
