## Discovery
- **Policy chosen:** 2022 Russian gas cutoff → import substitution — first-order economic shock with unexplored trade reallocation channel
- **Ideas rejected:** REACH 2018 (too weakly identified at country-sector level), Enterprise deaths (too few post-treatment years, annual data), BRRD bail-in (niche financial), EU Cohesion RDD (extends existing Becker et al. work)
- **Data source:** Eurostat Comext (monthly HS2 trade), STS_INPR_M (production), BD_SIZE (firm demography) — all open, no API keys
- **Key risk:** Separating import rerouting (replacing Russian suppliers) from genuine domestic-production substitution; addressed via partner decomposition and quantity-based analysis
