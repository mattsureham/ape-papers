\item \textit{Notes:}
\textbf{Country:} France.
\textbf{Research question:} Does closure of local tax offices (DRFIP/DDFIP) under France's 2019 NRP reform increase RN vote share?
\textbf{Policy mechanism:} The DGFiP consolidated its tax office network from 1,952 to 929 commune-level locations (2019--2024), removing the physical state tax presence from over 1,000 communes.
\textbf{Outcome definition:} RN/FN vote share (\%), RN votes divided by valid votes cast (\textit{exprim\'es}) at commune level.
