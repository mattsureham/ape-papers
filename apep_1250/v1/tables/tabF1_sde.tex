Notes: \textbf{Country:} United Kingdom. \textbf{Research question:} Did the January 2015 UK high-cost short-term credit price cap increase housing distress, measured by possession claims, in higher-payday-exposure areas of England and Wales? \textbf{Policy mechanism:} The cap limited HCSTC interest and fees to 0.8\% per day, imposed a \pounds 15 default-fee cap, and capped total repayment at 100\% of principal, sharply compressing access to payday credit.
