## Selection Decision (Required)

- **Selected idea title:** Does Insurance Make Markets Resilient? Flood Re and English Property Values
- **Why this is not duplicative:** No NBER working paper or top-journal publication studies the property market capitalization of Flood Re. The flood-risk-and-housing literature (Bin & Polasky 2004; Beltrán et al. 2018 JUE; Atreya et al. 2013) studies physical flood events or risk disclosure, not insurance access shocks. Flood Re is a unique quasi-experiment that changed insurance availability without changing physical risk.
- **Specific contribution delta (1-2 sentences):** This paper is the first to decompose the flood-risk property discount into an insurance-market-failure component vs. an actuarial-risk component, using Flood Re (April 2016) as a clean quasi-experiment with a built-in placebo (post-2009 builds ineligible for Flood Re). The triple-diff design with 24M+ transactions and dose-response by Council Tax band provides a novel test of whether making risk insurable restores market efficiency.
