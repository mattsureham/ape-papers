## Discovery
- **Idea selected:** idea_0919 — India's DMF mineral royalty redistribution, a compelling null-result story about the gap between revenue collection and actual development
- **Data source:** SHRUG v2.1 (Harvard Dataverse) — VIIRS nightlights needed separate download; DMSP on disk for supplementary pre-trends
- **Key risk:** Short pre-period (3 VIIRS years) and indirect treatment proxy (public mining employment vs actual DMF revenues)

## Execution
- **What worked:** Clean dose-response DiD design with 640 districts, flat pre-trends, strong narrative framing as "transfer trap"
- **What didn't:** DMSP calibration to extend pre-periods introduced spurious pre-trends; reverted to VIIRS-only main analysis
- **Review feedback adopted:** Toned down "precisely estimated null" language; added explicit acknowledgment of treatment proxy weakness and finite-sample inference concerns; added DMSP supplementary pre-trend analysis for extended pre-period validation
