## Discovery
- **Idea selected:** idea_1468 — Fair Workweek laws and the Black-White employment gap in food service
- **Data source:** QWI race/ethnicity panel from Azure — fetched cleanly, 248K rows for each industry
- **Key risk:** Progressive policy bundling in adopting jurisdictions

## Execution
- **What worked:** QWI DDD design constructed efficiently; construction placebo was the most informative test
- **What didn't:** The identification strategy collapsed under diagnostics — placebo failure, Oregon concentration, RI p=0.61
- **Review feedback adopted:** Added pre-trend evidence description, A0 nesting bias discussion, null mechanism emphasis, RI implementation details, Oregon policy bundle specifics, CS vs TWFE divergence explanation
- **Key lesson:** Cities that adopt Fair Workweek laws also adopt everything else. DDD cannot separate scheduling mandates from the progressive bundle without within-sector variation (firm-size thresholds, occupation exemptions)
