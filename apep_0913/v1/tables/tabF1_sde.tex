\item \textit{Notes:} \textbf{Country:} United States. \textbf{Research question:} Does federal wilderness designation under the 1964 Wilderness Act reduce tree cover loss at the legal boundary where commercial timber harvest transitions from prohibited to permitted? \textbf{Policy mechanism:} The Wilderness Act prohibits commercial timber harvesting, road building, and motorized vehicle use inside designated wilderness areas, while adjacent National Forest land permits managed timber harvest under USFS forest plans.
