## Key External Paper (SSRN, not NBER)

**Hosseini Maasoum & Lichtinger (2025)** "Generative AI as Seniority-Biased Technological Change: Evidence from U.S. Résumé and Job Posting Data" — SSRN 5425555. Uses proprietary résumé data (62M workers, 285K firms), firm-level "GenAI integrator" postings as treatment, within-firm junior vs. senior employment as outcome. Finds 7.7% junior headcount decline at adopting firms after 6 quarters; hiring channel, not separations. U-shaped education pattern.

**Our delta:** Entirely different data stack (SEC filings + BLS surveys vs. proprietary résumés), industry-level rather than firm-level design, occupation-level heterogeneity via O*NET Job Zones and AIOE scores, and multiple public-data cross-validation (QCEW + OEWS + CPS). This is an independent replication and extension using government administrative data, not a re-analysis of the same source.
