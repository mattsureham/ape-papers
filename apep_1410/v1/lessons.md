## Discovery
- **Idea selected:** idea_1309 — Swiss BVG conversion rate reform. Chosen for clean, formula-driven variation and direct behavioral outcome (annuity vs. capital choice).
- **Data source:** BFS Pensionskassenstatistik (PXWeb API) — no authentication required. 21 years of data (2004-2024).
- **Key risk:** Aggregate time-series design with only 21 observations limits statistical power and makes trend-confounding the main threat.

## Execution
- **What worked:** The BFS PXWeb API was reliable and well-documented. The fund-type heterogeneity analysis provided the strongest evidence for an institutional mechanism. The paradox framing ("annuity squeeze") gave the paper a clear hook.
- **What didn't:** OpenRouter API was down ("User not found"), blocking all empirics reviews. The disability placebo had a data quality issue (reporting change in 2010 created an artificial jump). The aggregate nature of the data prevented individual-level analysis.
- **Review feedback adopted:** Sharpened opening (lead with paradox), removed table codes from body text, punchier intensive margin interpretation, active voice improvements, stronger conclusion.
