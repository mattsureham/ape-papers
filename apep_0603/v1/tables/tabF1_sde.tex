\item \textit{Notes:} Standardized effect sizes (SDE) computed as $\hat{\beta} / \text{SD}(Y)$, where SD(Y) is the pre-treatment (2010--2015) standard deviation of each outcome across powiat-years. Research question: What is the local fiscal multiplier of Poland's Family 500+ unconditional cash transfer? Data: GUS BDL (Statistics Poland), 2010--2022.
