## Discovery
- **Idea selected:** idea_1520 — ACICS collapse and community college absorption
- **Data source:** IPEDS DuckDB (local copy) — accreditation columns NULL, pivoted to closure-based treatment
- **Key risk:** COVID contamination of post-2019 effects; pre-COVID results attenuated

## Execution
- **What worked:** The surprise finding (negative enrollment effect) made for a compelling paper. Clean event study pre-trends. Strong robustness across specifications.
- **What didn't:** Accreditation data unavailable in harmonized IPEDS — had to identify treatment through closures. The pre-COVID attenuation is a real weakness.
- **Review feedback adopted:** Added detailed event study coefficients, honest discussion of COVID confound, "bifurcation" interpretation of positive 4-year placebo, explicit limitations paragraph.
