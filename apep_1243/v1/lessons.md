## Discovery
- **Idea selected:** `idea_1694` because Swiss merger infrastructure already existed locally and the foreign-sorting angle was not yet published in the archive.
- **Data source:** BFS AGVCH merger registry plus BFS PXWeb population-by-citizenship table; the faster stock panel beat the richer migration-flow pull on runtime.
- **Key risk:** Voluntary merger selection and the need to harmonize predecessor communes into current successor boundaries without blurring treatment timing.

## Execution
- **What worked:** Recursive BFS code mapping to current boundaries and a narrow 2015--2020 treated window produced a valid V1 staggered-DiD design quickly.
- **What didn't:** The broader original design with failed merger votes, tax mechanisms, and longer pre-periods was too large for a one-shot V1.
- **Review feedback adopted:** Clarified the harmonization procedure, defined foreign residents as non-Swiss citizens, added a confidence-interval interpretation, and stated the anticipation/sample-scope caveats more directly.
