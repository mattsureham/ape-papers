## Discovery
- **Idea selected:** idea_1095 — EU beneficial ownership transparency reversal. The symmetric shock (AMLD5 adoption + CJEU reversal) matched tournament-winning patterns.
- **Data source:** Eurostat sts_rb_q (quarterly business registrations) — discovered during execution as superior to the originally planned annual enterprise births data
- **Key risk:** Country-level design with only 7 rolled-back countries, confounded by energy crisis

## Execution
- **What worked:** Eurostat quarterly data gave excellent temporal coverage (2015-2023) at the right frequency. Manufacturing placebo was the strongest diagnostic — transformed ambiguous reversal into clear macro interpretation.
- **What didn't:** Callaway-Sant'Anna failed due to all units being eventually treated within a narrow window. Pre-trends in adoption analysis were positive, forcing upper-bound interpretation.
- **Review feedback adopted:** Moved pre-trend discussion to main text, tempered proportionality claims, added composition-vs-quantity distinction. Deferred micro-data analysis (Companies House PSC) to V2.
