**Feedback:** The opening is disciplined and concrete. "India spent billions deploying 900,000 community health workers to the doorsteps of its poorest women" is a classic Shleifer hook—it uses a massive, tangible number to establish scale and human presence (Glaeser’s "doorsteps").
