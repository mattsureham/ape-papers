## Discovery
- **Idea selected:** idea_2183 — "Switching Paradox" tests FCA's CBA behavioral prediction using Google Trends
- **Data source:** Google Trends (free, weekly, UK-specific), FCA complaints (Excel download)
- **Key risk:** Small N (5 keywords) makes cluster-robust inference unreliable; proxy validity (search ≠ switching)

## Execution
- **What worked:** Clean data fetch, sharp treatment date, compelling paradox framing
- **What didn't:** Pre-trends are noisy; only 5 keywords means clustering is unreliable; ABI switching data unavailable programmatically
- **Review feedback adopted:** Added limitations section on small N and proxy validity; expanded event study interpretation to acknowledge pre-trends; added MSE contamination discussion; tempered causal language
