# Research Ideas

## Selected Idea: German Solar Bunching at 10 kWp (Revision of idea_1228)

**Question:** How does Germany's 10 kWp solar threshold distort installation sizes, and what happens when the threshold moves?

**Identification:** Kleven-Waseem bunching estimator with four-break design: no threshold (2008-2011), FIT kink (2012-2013), surcharge notch (2014-2020), post-reform (2021-2024).

**Data:** MaStR universe of 3M rooftop solar installations (2008-2024).

**Feasibility:** Confirmed. MaStR data downloaded, smoke test passed with 712:1 ratio at 9.9/10.1 kWp during surcharge period, collapsing to 3.7:1 post-reform.

**Why revise:** V1 used OPSD (2008-2018 only) and lacked the 2021 reversal test, mechanism evidence, and general framing. V2 adds MaStR through 2024, the four-break event study, module count analysis, and reframes around the general proposition about threshold-based policy under expert intermediation.
