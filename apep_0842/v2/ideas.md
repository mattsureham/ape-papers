# Research Ideas — apep_0842

## Selected Idea: The Designation Illusion

**Research Question:** Do EU safe country of origin designations causally affect asylum recognition rates, or do they operate through deterrence and diversion?

**Identification:** Triple difference-in-differences exploiting variation in national SCO lists across 22 EU destinations, 20 origin nationalities, and 16 years (2008-2023).

**Data:** Eurostat migr_asydcfsta (asylum decisions) + migr_asyappctza (applications) + AIDA SCO treatment matrix.

**Feasibility:** READY — all data publicly available, rich panel variation.
